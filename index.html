TURKEY

Turkey is a country that lies between Europe and Asia, rich in history and culture. Its largest city, Istanbul, was once known as Constantinople and served as the capital of both the Byzantine and Ottoman Empires. Turkey is famous for its beautiful landscapes, delicious food like kebabs and baklava, and historic sites such as Hagia Sophia and Cappadocia. With a blend of modern life and ancient traditions, Turkey attracts millions of tourists each year.
